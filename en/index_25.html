The Problem: Bicycle lights need power, but carrying batteries adds weight and requires replacement.

TRIZ Solution: Make the system serve itself! The bicycle's wheel rotation generates its own electricity through a dynamo.

Self-Service Benefits:

  • No external batteries needed
  • Automatic operation - light intensity matches riding activity
  • Sustainable and maintenance-free power generation
  • The faster you pedal, the brighter your light becomes!

This principle appears in many systems: self-winding watches, regenerative braking in electric cars, solar-powered calculators, and automatic doors that generate their own power from usage.
